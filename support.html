FAQ

  • How do I report a bug?
    Send us a short description of the issue, and include your device model and iOS version if possible.
  • How do I send feedback?
    You can email us anytime with ideas, feedback, or suggestions for future updates.
  • What should I do if the game is not working properly?
    Try restarting the app and making sure you are using the latest available version.
  • How can I contact support?
    Email us directly at PerfectTap@oyaholding.com.
